October myMU Guide  

Congratulations on your acceptance to Messiah University for the spring 2021 semester!

Now is a great time to get familiar with myMU, Messiah's accepted student portal, where you will find important dates, forms, course registration information, and other helpful content.

The myMU Guide is a monthly email which will explain what's new on the portal and help keep you on track to start at Messiah this spring.

This month's issue features dining services, financial aid, and residence life.

 
Activate Your myMU Account
Using your student ID number found in your acceptance packet, please activate your myMU account, if you have not already done so. And be sure to visit myMU often as new information is being added all the time. Remember to be checking your Messiah email account as well!

Content on myMU is in alphabetical order. The middle column shows the month content will be available, with deadlines listed on the right.

If you have trouble getting logged in, please email 
helpdesk@messiah.edu or call 717-796-5039 to reach Educational Technology Services.
 
Review Messiah's Meal Plan
Click on, "Dining Services" to review the Dining Service FAQ and learn about Messiah's meal plan. 

Coming Soon: The Spring 2021 Meal Plan Selection will become available on Nov. 2, 2020 via The Nest.

All students living in traditional housing (residence halls) will be automatically signed up for the Messiah meal plan. Commuters who wish to participate in the meal plan will need to opt in. 
Understand Your Financial Aid
Review the "Financial Aid" links on myMU for information on your financial aid award (available to review beginning approximately Nov. 1), in addition to other financial aid resources.

Remember: You'll receive an email to your Messiah email account when your award is ready for your review.

Important: To be considered for need-based financial aid, you and your family should file the Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA). This form is currently available for the 2020-2021 academic year, and should be filed as soon as possible.

Residence Life: All housing-related information will be located here on myMU. It is important for both students who will live on campus AND for students planning to commute.  
  • Learn how to access your Accepted Student Housing Contract and Application, housing preferences form and when finalized, your housing assignment.
